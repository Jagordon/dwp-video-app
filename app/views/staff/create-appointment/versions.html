2.0

Research findings
  • Majority of work coaches flicked between tabs to copy information between UC build and our services. One split the screen and two made a physical note of appointment details.
  • 5/6 work coaches understood how and where to copy and paste the code/link and attendee link between the two services. One needed guidance as had not scrolled down on UC to-do to see inputs.
  • 5/6 workcoaches used the 'copy' buttons.
  • Confusion over where to copy the help link. Some copied in to the attendee link box on the UC prototype. 2 others spoke of putting it into the claimants journal.
  • A few workcoaches pulled the tab out in to a new window and worked split screen whilst on the video appointment.
  • Often workcoaches had multiple tabs open and did not close our services once finished. One work coach got particularly lost in them.
  • 'Finish' button caused confusion over whether the user had done something wrong when it took them back to the start.
  • 'The quicker we can make the booking process the better'.
