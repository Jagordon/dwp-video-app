Allow access to your camera and microphone

If you're asked to allow use of your camera and microphone, you must choose 'Allow' to take part in the video appointment.

Did not see a message?
If you did not see a message and cannot see or hear the other person, you may need to change your browser settings:

Mobile

iPhone or iPad

1. Go to your 'Settings'.
2. Select 'Safari'.
3. Select 'Camera' or 'Microphone' and choose 'Allow'.

Android

1. Go to your 'Settings'.
2. Select 'Apps and notifications'.
3. Select 'Chrome' (select 'See all apps' if you cannot see it).
4. Select 'Permissions'.
5. Select 'Camera' or 'Microphone' and choose 'Allow'.

Desktop

Safari

1. Go to 'Safari'on your toolbar.
2. Select 'Preferences'.
3. Select 'Websites'.
4. Select 'Camera' or 'Microphone' and choose 'Allow'.

Chrome

1. At the top right of your browser, select the three dots.
2. Select 'Settings'.
3. In 'Advanced', select 'Privacy and security'.
4. Select 'Site settings'.
5. Select 'Camera' or 'Microphone' and choose 'Allow'.
