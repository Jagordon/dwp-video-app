Your camera or microphone is blocked

You will need to unblock access to your camera or microphone to take part in the video appointment. Follow the instructions for your device and browser.

Mobile

iPhone or iPad

  1. Go to your device 'Settings'.
  2. Select 'Safari'.
  3. Select 'Camera' or 'Microphone' and choose 'Ask'.
  4. Refresh your browser.

Android

  1. Go to your device 'Settings'.
  2. Select 'Apps'.
  3. Select 'Chrome'.
  4. Select 'Permissions'.
  5. Select 'Camera' or 'Microphone' and choose 'Allow'.

Desktop

Safari

  1. Go to 'Safari'on your toolbar.
  2. Select 'Preferences'.
  3. Select 'Websites'.
  4. Select the 'Camera' or 'Microphone' and choose 'Allow' for this website.

Chrome

  1. At the top right of your browser, select the three dots.
  2. Select 'Settings'.
  3. In 'Advanced', select 'Privacy and security'.
  4. Select 'Site settings'.
  5. Select the 'Camera' or 'Microphone' and delete this website from the 'Block' section.
